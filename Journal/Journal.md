## June 16th: Got the main design in place!

 For This project, I will be mainly using SolidWorks 2026

The idea is to make a simple rotating platform so I can scan real-life objects into 3d.

It will be servo-controlled by a 28BYJ-4, an Arduino Nano, and a potentiometer to control speed.
It's a fairly simple design that would allow me to learn more about 3D-printed gears.
For now, it would be composed of 2 parts:
- A base where all the electronics would stay and will have the servo with a spur gear on top of it.
- A platform that would have an internal spur gear on the bottom that will rotate due to the servo's gear movement.

I also added bearings for a smooth rotation with little friction.
